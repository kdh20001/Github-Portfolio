About Me

Home Lab & Technical Projects:
Technology has fascinated me since childhood. I maintain a personal home lab where I repair and repurpose electronics, transforming outdated devices into functional tools—such as converting old laptops into media centers or using spare routers as Wi-Fi extenders. I’ve built a personal cloud using salvaged HDDs and SSDs, and I regularly assist friends and family with troubleshooting and repairs. My philosophy is simple: “If you can’t fix it, you don’t own it.”
